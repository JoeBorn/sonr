SONR LABS API Rules & TOS

Draft June 9, 2012


SONR Labs Inc. (“SONR”) supports standard protocols for certain users behaviors relating to audio. We want to empower our partners to build businesses around the information flowing through SONR, but need to balance encouraging development with protecting SONR's and users' rights.


These Rules of the Road for Developers ("Rules") along with SONR’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policies, which are incorporated fully herein (collectively the “TOS”) are a condition of access to and use of the SONR API and SONR “Content” as described below. The Rules govern innovations that are permitted with the data and describe our approach to supporting a SONR developer ecosystem.


The Rules will evolve along with our ecosystem as developers continue to innovate new ways to use the SONR API, so please check back periodically for the updated version. If you have any questions or issues with these Rules, we’re happy to discuss suggested changes or consider an exception. If you will eventually need more than ___ user tokens for your projects, you will also need to talk to us directly about access.

II. Principles


We ask that you and your Service follow two principles: a) Respect user privacy and b) Be a good partner to SONR.


Respect user privacy - Your Service must display and comply with a privacy policy that clearly discloses what you are doing with information you collect from users. If your Service supports cookies, your privacy policy must disclose that third parties may be placing and reading cookies on the systems of your users in the course of providing content to them. Your privacy policy should also provide information about user options for cookie management and the Do Not Track setting in supporting web browsers. You should not solicit another developer's consumer keys or consumer secrets especially if they will be stored or used for actions outside of that developer's control. Do not facilitate or encourage the publishing of private or confidential information.


Be a good partner to SONR - Respect the features and functionality embedded with or included in SONR Content or the SONR API. Do not attempt to interfere with, intercept, disrupt, filter, or disable any features of the SONR API or SONR service, and you should only surface actions that are organically displayed on SONR. SONR may suspend or revoke access if we believe you are in violation of the Rules or the spirit of these principles. If you are suspended, do not apply for or register additional API tokens.


III. SONR Functionality and Use


SONR Login - End users must be presented with the option to log into SONR. End users without a SONR account should be given the opportunity to create a new SONR account as provided by SONR. You must display the Connect with SONR option at least as prominently as the most prominent of any other third party sign-up or sign-in marks and branding appearing on you Service.


Commercial Use - Our goal is to provide you, our ecosystem partner, with a policy that is clear and transparent about what you can do to monetize your Service. SONR reserves the right to serve advertising via its APIs ("SONR Ads"). Advertising Around SONR Content. We encourage you to create advertising opportunities around SONR content that are compliant with these Rules. In cases where SONR Content is the primary basis of the advertising sale, we require you to compensate us (recoupable against any fees payable to SONR for data licensing). Get the users' permission before using their content on a commercial product
